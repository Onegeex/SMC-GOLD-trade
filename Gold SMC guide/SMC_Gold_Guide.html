What This Script Draws

🟩
Bullish Order Block
The last bearish candle before a strong bullish impulse. Institutions placed buy orders here. Price tends to return and react.
🟥
Bearish Order Block
The last bullish candle before a strong bearish impulse. Smart money placed sell orders here — a high-probability short zone.
🔷
Bullish FVG
A gap between candles where no trading occurred (candle 1 high < candle 3 low). Price is drawn back to fill the imbalance before continuing up.
🔶
Bearish FVG
Downside imbalance gap. Price fills it from below before resuming the sell-off. Used as a short entry zone on pullbacks.
💧
Liquidity Sweep
Price spikes above a recent high or below a recent low to grab stop losses, then reverses. This is the setup trigger — the most important signal.
🕗
Kill Zones
London (08–11 UTC) and New York (13–16 UTC) open windows. SMC signals are only valid inside these zones when Gold is institutionally active.

Kill Zones Explained

08:00 – 11:00
London Kill Zone · UTC
London open is where Gold makes its first major move of the day. Institutions place orders as European markets open, creating sharp directional moves. Most reliable for trend continuation setups.
13:00 – 16:00
New York Kill Zone · UTC
NY open overlaps with London, bringing maximum liquidity. Gold often reverses or accelerates here. The most volatile window — ideal for liquidity sweep setups and reversals off order blocks.
🕐 Nigeria Time (WAT = UTC+1): London Kill Zone = 09:00–12:00 WAT · New York Kill Zone = 14:00–17:00 WAT. Set your TradingView timezone to UTC for the script to highlight correctly.

How To Install

1

Open XAUUSD H1 Chart

Go to TradingView, search XAUUSD, set the timeframe to 1H. This is your primary trading chart.

2

Open Pine Editor

Click Pine Editor at the bottom of the TradingView interface. Delete any existing code in the editor.

3

Paste the Script

Open the XAUUSD_SMC_Strategy.pine file, copy all the code, paste it into the Pine Editor, then click Add to chart.

4

Set Timezone to UTC

In TradingView chart settings → Timezone → select UTC. This ensures Kill Zone shading is accurate for Gold session hours.

5

Check Higher Timeframe First

Before taking any H1 signal, switch to H4 or D1 and confirm the overall structure direction. Only trade H1 signals that align with the higher timeframe bias.

6

Set Alerts (Optional)

Right-click chart → Add Alert → choose SMC BUY — XAUUSD or SMC SELL — XAUUSD. You'll get notified instantly when a setup fires, even when you're away from the screen.
